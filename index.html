Tasty and Spicy Food Menu

Appetizers

  • Spicy Buffalo Cauliflower Bites: Toss cauliflower florets in a mixture of hot sauce, melted butter, and spices like paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper
  • Stuffed Jalapeño Poppers: Our signature Stuffed Jalapeño Poppers are a delightful combination of heat and creaminess, perfect for sharing or savoring solo. Fresh jalapeño peppers are halved and filled with a rich and savory mixture, then baked to golden perfection. Each bite offers a tantalizing blend of flavors and textures, with a kick of spice balanced by creamy goodness.
  • Spicy Thai Shrimp Lettuce Wraps: Embark on a culinary journey with our vibrant and flavorful Spicy Thai Shrimp Lettuce Wraps. Bursting with bold Thai-inspired spices and fresh ingredients, these wraps are a tantalizing explosion of taste in every bite. Succulent shrimp marinated in a zesty Thai red curry sauce are nestled in crisp lettuce leaves, then adorned with a medley of crunchy vegetables and fragrant herbs. The combination of heat, sweetness, and tanginess will invigorate your taste buds and leave you craving more.

Main Courses

  • Spicy Szechuan Chicken Stir-Fry: Prepare your taste buds for a culinary adventure with our Spicy Szechuan Chicken Stir-Fry. Tender strips of chicken breast are wok-tossed to perfection in a fiery Szechuan sauce that's as bold as it is flavorful. Each bite bursts with the robust essence of traditional Szechuan spices, including chili paste, garlic, and ginger, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on your palate. Accompanied by a colorful medley of bell peppers, onions, and broccoli, this dish offers a tantalizing balance of heat and freshness.
  • Spicy Jambalaya: Indulge in the soulful flavors of the South with our Spicy Jambalaya, a hearty and vibrant dish that captures the essence of Louisiana cuisine. Our signature recipe features a tantalizing blend of Andouille sausage, plump shrimp, and tender chicken, all simmered together with aromatic vegetables and long-grain rice. Infused with a spicy kick from a special blend of Cajun seasoning, cayenne pepper, and hot sauce, each spoonful offers a symphony of bold and fiery flavors. Served piping hot, our Jambalaya is sure to transport you to the bustling streets of New Orleans with its irresistible Cajun flair.
  • Spicy Thai Basil Beef: Experience the vibrant and aromatic flavors of Thailand with our Spicy Thai Basil Beef. Tender slices of beef are wok-fried to perfection with fragrant garlic, fiery chili peppers, and fresh Thai basil leaves, creating a tantalizing symphony of tastes and textures. Our signature sauce, crafted from a blend of soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, and a hint of sweetness, coats each morsel of beef, infusing it with a savory umami richness. Served with steamed jasmine rice, this dish offers a harmonious balance of heat, spice, and herbal freshness that will transport your taste buds to the bustling streets of Bangkok.

Desserts

  • Spicy Chocolate Chili Brownie Sundae: Indulge your senses with our Spicy Chocolate Chili Brownie Sundae, a decadent treat that combines rich chocolate brownie chunks with a hint of fiery chili heat. Topped with a scoop of velvety vanilla ice cream, drizzled with warm chocolate sauce, and finished with a sprinkle of spicy chili flakes, this dessert is sure to delight your taste buds.
  • Mango Chili Lime Sorbet: Refresh your palate with our Mango Chili Lime Sorbet, a tropical sensation with a spicy twist. Made with ripe mangoes and zesty lime juice, this tangy sorbet is infused with a subtle kick of chili heat, creating a harmonious blend of flavors that dance on your tongue. Served chilled and garnished with fresh mint leaves, it's the perfect way to cleanse your palate after a delicious meal.
  • Thai Basil-infused Pineapple Carpaccio: Elevate your dessert experience with our Thai Basil-infused Pineapple Carpaccio, a light and refreshing treat inspired by Thai cuisine. Thinly sliced pineapple is marinated in a delicate syrup infused with fragrant Thai basil, imparting a subtle herbal flavor. Served chilled and garnished with toasted coconut flakes and a sprig of fresh basil, it's a sophisticated finale to your dining experience.
